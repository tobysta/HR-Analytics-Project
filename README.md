# HR Analytics Power BI Dashboard

HR Analytics Power BI Project analyzes employee data to uncover insights on attrition, job satisfaction, and salary trends across departments. This interactive dashboard features three tabs with KPIs, slicers, and visualizations for comprehensive HR decision-making.

## Features:

### **1. Department Performance Dashboard**
**KPIs:** Employee count, Average age, Average total working hours  
**Slicers:** Department, Gender  

**Visualizations:**
- Attrition by Department
- Headcount by Department & Gender
- Employees by Job Role
- Headcount by Department & Education
- Business Travel by Department & Age Group

### **2. Job Satisfaction Dashboard**
**KPIs:** Employee count, Average age, Average total working hours  
**Slicers:** Department, Gender  

**Visualizations:**
- Job Involvement by Age 
- Employees by Work Environment Satisfaction
- Employee Distribution by Job Level
- Count of employees by Job Satisfaction
- Workforce by Marital Status & Gender

### **3. Compensation Analysis Dashboard**
**KPIs:** Monthly income sum, Employee count, Average age, Average working hours, Average companies worked  
**Slicers:** Department, Gender, Age Group, Age  

**Visualizations:**
- Top 10 Employee Income Distribution
- Income by Job Role
- Employees by Salary Slab
- Income by Job Level

## Usage Instructions:

1. Download the `.pbix` file from the repository
2. Open in **Power BI Desktop**
3. Use slicers to filter data dynamically

## Technologies Used:
- **Power BI** (Power Query)
- **HR Analytics Dataset**
